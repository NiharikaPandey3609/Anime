In Japan, anime is the term used to refer to all animations. And for the rest of the world, anime is the umbrella term for an animation from Japan. It has a wide pool of fans, ranging from kids to adults. Anime is more than just childhood thing. It influences you in so many ways that it becomes something you don't just "grow out of". It's quite unique from the everyday cartoon when it comes to details, styles and storytelling.

WHAT IS ANIME?

Anime, sometimes called Japanimation is hand drawn and computer animations which refers to all kind of animated media. Outside Japan, anime specially refers to animation from Japan or as a Japanese-disseminated animation style often characterised by colourful graphics, vibrant characters amd fantastical themes.

The culturally abstract approach to the words meaning may open up the possiblity of anime produced in countries other than Japan.

Many Westerners strictly view anime as a Japanese animation product. Some scholars suggest defining anime as a specificially or quintessentially Japanese maybe related to a new form of orientation.
The earliest commercial that Japanese animation dates to 1917, and Japanese anime production has since continued to increase steadily.The characterstic anime art style emerged in the 1960's.

The anime industry consists of over 430 production studios, including major names like Studio Ghibli, Gianax and Toie Animation.In 2015 the 44th President of the United States, Barak Obama, reffered anime as one of the most loved ones of the American youth.
