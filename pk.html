Pokémon (ポケモン) is one of the most popular Japanese anime and games around the globe now, and it was first aired in Japan as the Japanese title Pocket Monsters (ポケットモンスター) in 1997. New titles have been broadcasted every time a new game is released, and the latest series is being broadcasted from 2019 following the previous Sun & Moon (ポケットモンスター サン&ムーン). The young boy Satoshi travels all over the world with various Pokemon like Pikachu to become a Pokemon master.



Air Dates: from 1997
Genre: Kids, Family, Adventure
Recommended for Beginners
